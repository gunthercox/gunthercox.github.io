Welcome to the blog. This is where I'll share notes on things I'm building, problems I've solved, and tools I find useful across software, robotics, and hardware.

## What to Expect

Posts here will cover:

- **Open source** — development notes, release highlights, and lessons from maintaining public projects
- **Robotics** — hardware experiments, software integrations, and build logs
- **Engineering** — approaches to building reliable systems and practical tooling

Posts will be practical and specific. No filler.

## How This Blog Works

Posts are written in Markdown and built with [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/) alongside a Tailwind CSS-based design system. The site is served as static HTML with no client-side JavaScript required.

You can subscribe via [RSS](/feed.xml) to follow new posts.
